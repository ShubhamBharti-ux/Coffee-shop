Our Story

Founded in 2025, Buddies Coffee Shop started with a simple idea: to serve amazing coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. We believe coffee is more than a drink — it's a daily ritual, a way to connect, and a source of inspiration.

Our mission is to bring people together, one cup at a time. We proudly brew with locally roasted beans, serve fresh pastries daily, and offer a cozy space where friends become family.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to read, catch up on work, or just enjoy a cappuccino with a friend — Bharti’s Coffee Shop is your neighborhood hideaway.
